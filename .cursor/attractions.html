Top Attractions in Rio de Janeiro

Christ the Redeemer

Standing 98 feet tall atop Corcovado Mountain, this iconic statue is one of the New Seven Wonders of the World. The journey to the top offers panoramic views of Rio's beaches, mountains, and cityscape. Whether you take the scenic train ride or hike through Tijuca Forest, reaching this monument is an unforgettable experience that captures the spirit of Rio.

Sugarloaf Mountain

Take a thrilling cable car ride 1,299 feet above Guanabara Bay to witness breathtaking 360-degree views of Rio. The two-stage journey stops at Morro da Urca before reaching the summit. Perfect for sunrise or sunset, Sugarloaf offers unmatched photo opportunities of the city's dramatic landscape where mountains meet the ocean.

Copacabana Beach

This world-famous 2.5-mile stretch of golden sand is the heart of Rio's beach culture. Walk along the iconic black and white wave-patterned promenade, watch beach volleyball matches, or simply relax with a fresh coconut water. By night, the beachfront comes alive with bars, restaurants, and the energy that defines Rio's social scene.
